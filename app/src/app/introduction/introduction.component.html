Welcome

Ariton is different than your average app. You don't need to register to sign up, Ariton has already made a new account for you.

If you want to keep your account, you can take a backup of it. This will allow you to use your account on other devices, and if you delete your browser data, you can restore it.

We also suggest you start protecting your account by securing it with your personal password. Right now anyone who have access to your device, can access your Ariton account.
